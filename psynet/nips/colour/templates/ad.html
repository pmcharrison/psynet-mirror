Call for participants

The Max Planck Institute for Empirical Aesthetics is looking for online participants for a brief psychology experiment. In this study we want to understand people's internal representations of colour. The only requirements are as follows:

  • You should be at least 18 years old.
  • You should be a fluent English speaker.
  • You should be sitting in a quiet environment.
  • You should use a Google Chrome browser.

The task should take approximately minutes, and you will be paid at a rate of $ per hour. This is broken down into a base payment of $ and a bonus of $. You will also receive a performance bonus of up to $1.00 depending on how well you perform in the task.

The HIT may finish early if you fail performance checks or if we have no more data to collect from you. In this case you will still be compensated according to your progress through the experiment so far.

The HIT will begin with a short screening test to check that you are using headphones and can hear properly (2 minutes). If you fail this screening test, the HIT will end, and you will only be eligible for a small part of the bonus.

Note: please make sure you are using an up-to-date version of Google Chrome before accepting this HIT.
