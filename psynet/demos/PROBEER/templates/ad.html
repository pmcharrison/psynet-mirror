Call for participants

The Max Planck Institute for Empirical Aesthetics is looking for online participants for a brief psychology experiment. In this study we want to understand how people express intentions with their voice. The only requirements are as follows:

  • You must be wearing headphones.
  • You should be at least 18 years old.
  • You should be a fluent English speaker.
  • You should be sitting in a quiet environment.
  • You should use a google Chrome browser.

During the experiment you can use a slider that allows you to change the voice of someone saying the word beer. Your task will be to change the slider so the word sounds like a suggestion or like a criticism. The task should take approximately {{ duration_minutes }} minutes, and you will be paid at a rate of ${{ wage_per_hour }} per hour. The HIT will begin with a short screening test to check that you are using headphones and can hear properly (2 minutes). If you fail this screening test, the HIT will end, and you will only be eligible for the base payment.
