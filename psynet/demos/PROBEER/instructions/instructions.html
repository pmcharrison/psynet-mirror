During the experiment you will listen to the word 'beer'. Your task is to make the word sound like a criticism or a suggestion. You can modify the voice by moving the slider. Differences between the slider positions may be subtle, so listen carefully and try your best. You do not need to memorize the slider position, just move the slider to convey the given intention.
The experiment consists of two parts, you will with start with part one on the next page.

During the experiment we will monitor the consistency of your responses in the task. If we notice anomalies during the experiment, this will lead to exclusion.
