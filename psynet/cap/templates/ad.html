Call for participants

The Max Planck Institute for Empirical Aesthetics is looking for online participants for a brief psychology experiment. In this study we want to understand people's internal representations of faces. The only requirements are as follows:

  • You should be at least 18 years old.
  • You should be a fluent English speaker.
  • You must have no problems with your colour vision.
  • You should be sitting in a quiet environment.
  • You should use a Google Chrome browser.

We estimate that the task should take approximately minutes. Upon completion of the full task, you should receive a bonus of $ to $ depending on your performance in the task.

In some cases, the experiment may finish early: this is not an error, and there is no need to write to us. In this case you will be paid in proportion to the amount of the experiment that you completed.

Note: please make sure you are using an up-to-date version of Google Chrome before accepting this HIT.
